How to Play

  • Move your paddle UP and DOWN using the Arrow Keys or W/S Keys
  • First player to score 7 points wins the game
  • Press SPACE to pause/resume the game
  • Change difficulty to make the game easier or harder
  • Try to bounce the ball at angles to make it harder for the computer
